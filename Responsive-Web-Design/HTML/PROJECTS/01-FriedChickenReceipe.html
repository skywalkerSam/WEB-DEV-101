Fried Chicken

Cook a delicious Black Pepper Fried Chicken meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Black Pepper, Cumin & Salt
  • Garlic
  • Onions, and other veggies of your choice

Instructions

  1. Wash and pat dry chicken pieces
  2. Warm up the pan, and add some oil of your choice
  3. Add Cumin, Garlic, Veggies, and Chicken pieces
  4. Fry until browned, add Black Pepper and Salt, and slow cook until they're tender and ready to eat.
